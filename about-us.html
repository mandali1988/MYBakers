A wonderful aroma of the freshly baked goods ready to revive and refresh the taste buds of food lovers...is the everyday story at My Bakers. It is one of oldest, trusted and loved name in the bakery industry as a leading distributor of the quality baked products, also having own retail outlets across Ahmedabad. Spearheaded by Mr. Mukesh Chawla, My Bakers was incepted in the year ...with the passion to herald tasty innovations in the bakery business. With an experienced team of bakers. My Bakers is operating its Medium - Scale bakery business through state of an art production unit equipped with advanced and automated machinery to ensure the highest levels of hygiene, purity, freshness and quality for the products.

My Bakers baked a delightful range of products that include Toast, Khari, Cream Roll, Cake, slice Cake, Cookies, Biscuits, Nankhatai, Snacks item and more. Each product bears the stamp of unadulterated joy that My Bakers intends to achieve through its amazing bakery Products. Operating both as a distribution partner and My Bakers' retail bakery outlets and ready to surprise the customers with something new every time!
